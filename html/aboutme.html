Strong Coffee, Strong Roots All About Me

Hi! My name is Lydia King. I am a 21-year-old college sophomore at Brigham Young University in Provo, Utah. I come from the most beautiful city in the whole world, Coeur d'Alene, Idaho. If you are interested in learning more about Coeur d'Alene, check out the link below, which will take you to the website for my town's famous golf and spa resort.

I love to go paddleboarding on our lake in Coeur d'Alene, and in the summers I help my dad run our family paddleboard and jetski rental business. My family is one of the most important things to me in the entire world. I am the oldest of 7 children, and we are all very close. Religion is also incredibly important to me, enough so that I dedicated a year and a half of my life to do a service mission for my church, The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints! I love my life and couldn't ask for better.
